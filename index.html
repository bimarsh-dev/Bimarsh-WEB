About

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on 5 February 1985, is a Portuguese professional footballer widely considered one of the greatest players of all time.

Career

He has played for Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al Nassr, breaking records everywhere he goes.
